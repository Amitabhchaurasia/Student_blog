Healthy Food Options for Busy College Students

Too many snacks, large meal portions, decreased activity—these poor habits are not only unhealthy, they may affect academic performance. A study from the National Institutes of Health found a correlation between eating regular meals, including breakfast and a higher consumption of fruit, with higher academic achievement. Another study from the Journal of American College Health found that students' current GPA increased significantly with the number of days that they reported eating breakfast.Healthy food is an important factor in academic success.
